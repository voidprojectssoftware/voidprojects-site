We build AI-centric tools as a small developer collective, and this is where we
write about it — the design decisions, the dead ends, and the occasional thing
that actually worked.

## What we're working on

Three projects are in flight right now:

- **Constellation** — turning amorphic systems into navigable graphs of knowledge.
- **Protostar** — agent skills that get better the more you use them.
- **Wormhole** — querying a teammate's local notes from your own agent harness.

## What to expect here

Mostly engineering write-ups. Some will be deep (the homepage physics engine is
its own rabbit hole), some will be short notes. If it taught us something, it's
fair game.

> The void isn't empty. It's full of the things we haven't named yet.

Thanks for reading.
